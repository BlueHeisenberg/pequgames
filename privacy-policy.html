1. Introduction

PeQu Games develops and publishes mobile games for iOS and Android. This privacy policy explains how we handle information in connection with the following apps:

  • Tap to Exist

  • Bigger Eats Smaller
  • One Bullet
  • Hold Your Breath
  • Wrong Direction
  • Paint the Floor

By using any of our apps, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Our apps do not directly collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not require account creation, and we do not ask for your name, email address, or any other identifying details within the apps themselves.

3. Advertising

Our apps display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information automatically to serve and personalize ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General device information (model, operating system, screen size)
  • App usage data and interaction with advertisements

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with their own privacy policies. You can manage your ad personalization preferences through your device settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Our apps use the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob - for displaying advertisements. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

We encourage you to review Google's privacy policy to understand how your data may be collected and used by their advertising services.

5. Children's Privacy

Our games are intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child under 13 has provided personal information through one of our apps, please contact us at the email address below and we will take steps to remove that information.

6. Data Security

While our apps do not collect personal data directly, we are committed to ensuring that any information handled through third-party services is protected. We use commercially reasonable measures to safeguard the integrity of our apps and work with trusted third-party partners who maintain their own security standards.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we handle information.
